About Me

Dedicated to Healing Bones, Changing Lives

"Surgery is an art as much as a science ‚Äî precision of the hand guided by wisdom of the mind."

Dr. Saleem Akram is a PMDC-registered Orthopedic Surgeon practicing at National Hospital & Medical Centre, DHA Lahore. With over 25 years of experience in orthopedic and reconstructive surgery, he brings deep expertise in brachial plexus injuries, congenital deformities, pediatric orthopedics, and sports injuries. He is also a recognized medical author and educator, contributing to both Urdu and English medical literature.

Areas of Expertise

Surgical Specialties & Clinical Focus

Comprehensive orthopedic care with a focus on complex and reconstructive procedures, combining surgical excellence with personalized patient treatment plans.

Brachial Plexus Surgery

Advanced reconstructive surgery for brachial plexus injuries including birth palsy, traumatic injuries, and nerve repair techniques.

Shoulder & Upper Limb

Treatment of recurrent shoulder dislocation, rotator cuff injuries, CP hand, and complex upper extremity conditions.

Pediatric Orthopedics

Specialized care for congenital hip dislocation, radial club hand, birth-related deformities, and childhood bone disorders.

Tendon & Nerve Repair

Microsurgical repair of tendons and peripheral nerves, restoring function and mobility to the hand and upper extremity.

Sports Injuries

Comprehensive management of sports-related orthopedic injuries including ligament tears, stress fractures, and joint disorders.

Medical Education

Prolific author of medical texts in Urdu and English, and active educator training the next generation of orthopedic surgeons in Pakistan.
